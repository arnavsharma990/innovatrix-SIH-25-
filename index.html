The Challenge

Charting the Undiscovered

The deep ocean harbors a vast share of Earthâ€™s biodiversity, yet much of it remains undiscovered. Understanding this life is crucial for ecology, conservation, and discovering novel species.

Environmental DNA (eDNA) is a powerful tool to study these hidden ecosystems, but its potential is limited by a critical gap: deep-sea life is severely underrepresented in genetic reference databases.

Incomplete Data

Standard tools misclassify reads, underestimating true biodiversity.

Computational Cost

Traditional analysis of massive eDNA datasets is slow and inefficient.

The Solution

An AI-Powered Discovery Engine

Database Independence

Our AI minimizes reliance on incomplete references, analyzing raw reads directly.

Accelerated Analysis

Optimized workflows reduce computational time from weeks to hours.

Novel Taxa Discovery

Identifies previously unknown species and lineages with high accuracy.

BioLine is an AI-driven eDNA analysis pipeline that leverages deep and unsupervised learning. It classifies and annotates sequences directly from raw data, overcoming the limitations of traditional methods.

This approach ensures more robust biodiversity monitoring, paving the way for improved conservation strategies.
